About Us

Family's of Color is Non-Profit organization which was founded by Rhonda Kirksey in 2006 with the sole aim of giving back to the Community.inother to Achieve our Aim,we have partnered with the Food bank in providing for foods for our community

Our Mission

At Family of Color, we embark on a heartfelt journey to eradicate hunger and poverty, infusing every action with compassion and commitment.

Our Vision

A world where no one goes to bed hungry, where communities thrive, and individuals are empowered to sculpt their destinies.
